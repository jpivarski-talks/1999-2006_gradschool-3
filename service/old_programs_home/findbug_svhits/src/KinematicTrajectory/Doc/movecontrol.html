The KTMoveControl Class

Introduction

KTMoveControl is a helper class for the basic track classes in the CLEO3 infrastructure. KTHelix and KTMoveable (and its descendents) use it in situations that require iterative movement to reach a desired point, such as the intersection with an arbitrary plane or the point of closest approach to a stereo wire. In that case, the helix is approximated by a parabola and the track is swum towards the final point in a series of steps, where the maximum step size, the direction of the steps and the convergence of the iteration process are controlled by KTMoveControl.
